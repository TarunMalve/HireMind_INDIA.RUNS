Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about the technology or integration? We have answers.

How does HireMind AI evaluate adjacent skills?

Our machine learning engine has analyzed thousands of technical frameworks. If a candidate is an expert in Vue.js and has built micro-frontends, our system translates that capability into React concepts. It recognizes structural mastery rather than focusing on exact keyword syntax.

How does the anti-cheat verification work?

When a candidate submits a claim, our system randomly generates three brief questions based on real-world edge cases. These questions are time-limited to prevent external lookup. This validates core concept understanding and provides a verified badge upon success.

Can I connect HireMind AI with Greenhouse or Lever?

Yes. Scale and Enterprise plans integrate directly with major ATS systems (Greenhouse, Lever, Workday) using API tokens. You can sync candidates automatically and see their Evidence Profiles directly inside your existing workspace.

What is the "Growth Velocity" score?

Growth Velocity measures how rapidly a candidate moves from basic proficiency to complex contributions in their career history. Candidates with high growth velocity transition into senior, cross-functional leadership roles significantly faster.
