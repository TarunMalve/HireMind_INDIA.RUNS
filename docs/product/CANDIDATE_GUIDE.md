## Getting Started

HireMind Elite gives you more than job matching — it gives you **career intelligence**. Your profile isn't just a resume; it's a living, AI-analyzed portrait of your capabilities, growth trajectory, and potential.

### What You Need
- A valid email address or Google/GitHub account
- Your resume (PDF or text)
- 10–15 minutes to complete your profile

---

## Registration

### Step 1 — Create Your Account

1. Go to the HireMind platform URL
2. Click **Get Started as Candidate** or **Sign Up**
3. Complete authentication via Clerk:
   - Email + password
   - Google OAuth
   - GitHub OAuth
4. When prompted for role, select **Candidate**

### Step 2 — Initial Profile Setup

After registering, you're taken to a profile setup screen:

1. Enter your **Professional Headline** (e.g., `Full-Stack Engineer | 5 Years Experience`)
2. Add your **Skills** — comma-separated tags (these are critical for matching!)
3. Add **Work Experience** entries
4. Add **Education** entries
5. (Optional) Add your **LinkedIn URL** and **GitHub URL**

> **Tip**: Adding LinkedIn and GitHub URLs gives you a +5 point credibility boost in your P(Legitimate) score.

## Resume Upload

### Why Upload a Resume?

Uploading your resume lets the AI:
- Extract and verify your stated skills
- Parse your experience timeline automatically
- Calculate your years of experience per role
- Generate your initial Candidate DNA profile

### How to Upload

1. From your **Candidate Dashboard**, click **Upload Resume**
2. Select your PDF file (or drag and drop)
3. Click **Submit**
4. Wait for the AI processing spinner (usually 5–10 seconds)

### What Gets Extracted

| Data | Extracted From |
|---|---|
| Skills | Job titles, technology mentions, bullet points |
| Experience | Role titles, company names, duration strings |
| Education | Degree, institution, graduation year |
| Summary | Executive summary or intro paragraph |
| Links | LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio URLs |

### After Upload

Review the extracted data in your profile. Correct any errors — for example, if the AI misread a job duration or missed a skill.

---

## AI Resume Review

After uploading, the AI provides an automated review of your resume:

### What the AI Analyzes

- **Skill coverage**: Which skills you've listed vs. what's typically required for your stated roles
- **Experience gaps**: Missing or unclear time periods
- **Seniority signals**: Whether your experience aligns with your stated headline
- **Profile completeness**: Fields that are missing that impact your ranking

### Where to View It

From your dashboard, click **AI Review** or **Resume Insights**.

## Building Your Profile

A complete profile dramatically improves your hire probability scores across multiple dimensions.

### Profile Sections

#### Skills

Add all technologies, tools, and frameworks you know:

```
React, TypeScript, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Docker, AWS, Python, Git
```

- Use common, recognizable names
- Don't keyword-stuff — the AI detects adjacent skills, so honesty is rewarded
- List skills in order of proficiency (strongest first)

#### Work Experience

For each role, include:
- **Title**: Your actual job title
- **Company**: Company name
- **Duration**: e.g., `3 yr 6 mo` or `2020–2023`
- **Highlights**: Key achievements and responsibilities (2–4 bullets)

Good example:
```
Senior Software Engineer — Acme Corp
Duration: 3 yr
Highlights:
- Led backend migration from monolith to microservices
- Reduced API response time by 40%
- Mentored 3 junior engineers
```

#### Education

- **Degree**: B.Tech Computer Science, MBA, etc.
- **Institution**: University name
- **Year**: Graduation year

#### Portfolio URLs

Add links to:
- GitHub profile
- Personal website / portfolio
- Notable projects
- Open-source contributions

> These feed your P(Scrappiness) score — the more evidence of initiative, the higher you rank.

## Career DNA

Your **Career DNA** is a multi-dimensional profile of your capabilities, generated by the AI.

### DNA Dimensions

| Dimension | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| **Technical Depth** | Engineering sophistication |
| **Problem Solving** | Analytical reasoning |
| **Leadership** | Team influence and ownership |
| **Communication** | Clarity and articulation |
| **Creativity** | Novel approaches |
| **Adaptability** | Flexibility across contexts |
| **Domain Expertise** | Specialization depth |
| **Growth Trajectory** | Career advancement velocity |

### Viewing Your DNA

1. From your dashboard, click **Career DNA**
2. View the **Radar Chart** — your capability shape
3. Review your **AI-generated strengths** and **growth areas**

### Interpreting Your DNA

- **Balanced radar** (all dimensions ≥ 0.65): Well-rounded generalist
- **Spiked in 2–3 dimensions**: Specialist profile — excellent for focused roles
- **Low in leadership + communication**: Strong IC signal — ideal for deep technical roles

### Improving Your DNA

| Dimension | How to Improve |
|---|---|
| Technical Depth | Work on complex system design projects |
| Leadership | Volunteer for team lead / mentoring roles |
| Communication | Contribute to documentation, blogs, talks |
| Growth Trajectory | Upskill consistently; update your profile regularly |
| Scrappiness | Contribute to open-source; build side projects |

## Authenticity Challenge

### What Is It?

The Authenticity Challenge is a set of open-ended questions that verify your claimed expertise.

### Why It Matters

Completing the challenge and scoring well increases your **P(Legitimate)** score, moving you higher in rankings and giving recruiters confidence in your profile.

### How to Complete

1. From your dashboard, click **Take Verification Challenge**
2. Answer 3 domain-specific questions related to your skills
3. Submit your responses
4. Your status updates to `PENDING → VERIFIED` (or `FAILED`)

### Sample Questions

```
1. "Explain a complex technical challenge you solved recently. 
    What was your approach and what did you learn?"

2. "What design patterns do you commonly use and why? 
    Give a specific example from your work."

3. "Describe your approach to debugging a production issue 
    under pressure. Walk us through your process."
```

### Challenge Status

| Status | Meaning | Score Impact |
|---|---|---|
| `PENDING` | Not yet completed | Baseline credibility |
| `VERIFIED` | Completed and passed | High P(Legitimate) |
| `FAILED` | Responses insufficient | Reduced P(Legitimate) |
| `CHALLENGED` | Under review | Pending |

## Learning Recommendations

HireMind generates personalized learning roadmaps based on your profile gaps and career goals.

### Accessing Your Roadmap

From your dashboard, click **Learning Roadmap** or **My Growth Plan**.

### Roadmap Structure

```
🎯 Goal: Senior Full-Stack Engineer
⏱️  Estimated: 16 weeks
📊 Progress: 25%

Milestones:
├── ✅ Week 1-4:  TypeScript Advanced Patterns
├── 🔄 Week 5-8:  PostgreSQL Performance (In Progress)
├── ⬜ Week 9-12: System Design Fundamentals
└── ⬜ Week 13-16: Open Source Contribution
```

### How Roadmaps Are Generated

Roadmaps are triggered by:
1. **Profile creation** — initial roadmap based on stated goals
2. **Application rejection** — "Second Chance" roadmap for near-miss candidates
3. **DNA analysis** — targeted improvements based on low DNA dimensions

## Interview Readiness

### Interview Readiness Score

Based on your profile completeness, DNA scores, and verification status, HireMind calculates an **Interview Readiness** indicator.

### Factors That Improve Readiness

- ✅ Resume uploaded and parsed
- ✅ All profile sections complete
- ✅ Authenticity challenge passed
- ✅ LinkedIn + GitHub linked
- ✅ Portfolio URLs added
- ✅ Skills accurately listed

### Preparing with Your DNA

Use your DNA radar to prepare for interviews:
- **Low communication score?** Practice STAR-format answers
- **Low technical depth?** Study system design fundamentals
- **Low leadership?** Prepare examples of initiative and ownership

## Application Tracking

### Submitting an Application

1. Browse jobs from the **Job Board** section
2. Click **Apply** on any active listing
3. Your profile is immediately scored against the job requirements
4. You're added to the candidate rankings for that job

### Application Status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `APPLIED` | Application submitted |
| `SCREENING` | Recruiter is reviewing your profile |
| `INTERVIEW` | Invited for an interview |
| `OFFER` | Offer extended |
| `HIRED` | Congratulations! |
| `REJECTED` | Not selected for this role |
| `SECOND_CHANCE` | Not selected, but a learning roadmap has been created |

### Viewing Your Status

From your dashboard, click **My Applications** to see:
- All active applications
- Current pipeline stage per job
- Your ranking score for each job

## Best Practices

> **Tip**: Be honest about your skills. The adjacency system gives you credit for equivalent technologies — you don't need to claim React if you know Vue.

> **Tip**: Complete your Authenticity Challenge as soon as it's available. Verified candidates rank significantly higher for the same technical score.

> **Tip**: Add your GitHub URL. Even a few public repositories dramatically improve your P(Scrappiness) score.

> **Tip**: If you receive a "Second Chance" status instead of a rejection, take the learning roadmap seriously. The recruiter saw potential in you — they're investing in your growth.

> **Tip**: Update your profile regularly. Stale profiles score lower on P(Engageable) because activity signals suggest you've stopped looking.
