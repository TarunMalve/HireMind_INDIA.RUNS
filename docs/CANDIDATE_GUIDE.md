# Candidate Portal Manual — HireMind Elite

Welcome to the Candidate Workspace guide. HireMind Elite allows you to understand how recruiters evaluate your profile and provides suggestions to boost your career prospects.

---

## 1. Onboarding & Resume Upload

When registering as a Candidate:
1.  Complete the 3-step registration wizard.
2.  Provide your profile details and paste or upload your latest resume.
3.  The system parser extracts your technical capabilities and builds your **Candidate DNA Profile**.

---

## 2. Navigating Your Dashboard

In the **Candidate Portal**, your workspace contains:
-  **Matching Opportunities**: Openings where your profile matches the employer's target DNA.
-  **Verify Your Claims**: Online validation quizzes tailored to your resume's stated technologies.
-  **My Career Roadmap**: A step-by-step training checklist suggesting certifications or open-source activities to complete.
-  **2-Year Role Prediction**: An AI analysis estimating your trajectory (e.g. progressing into an Architect role within 24 months).

---

## 3. Inline Credibility Quizzes

Recruiters prioritize verified candidates. To earn a **Verified Badge** and increase your match probability:
1.  Navigate to the **Verify Your Skill DNA** challenge box on your dashboard.
2.  The quiz presents multiple-choice questions testing claims on your resume (e.g. React hooks, GCP architecture).
3.  Click **Submit Answers**.
4.  If you answer correctly, you receive the **Verified Badge** (+30% match score boost as $P(Legitimate)$ updates to $1.00$).
5.  If you fail, you can try again after 24 hours, and your roadmap will update with suggested revision materials.
