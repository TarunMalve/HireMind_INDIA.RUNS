# System User Guide — HireMind Elite

Welcome to the HireMind Elite User Manual. This guide explains how to navigate and use the platform's visual components.

---

## 1. Landing Page Navigation

When you first load `http://localhost:3000` (or `index.html`), you are on the product landing page:
- **Navbar Links**: Feature highlights, How It Works breakdown, pricing grids, and about logs.
- **Theme Toggle Button**: Located in the upper right. Click to toggle between Dark mode and Light mode. The background, text colors, and cards will transition smoothly.
- **Get Started / Sign In**: Opens the overlay auth modals.

---

## 2. Authentication Modals

### Signing In
1.  Click **Sign In** on the navigation bar.
2.  Choose your portal type: **Recruiter** or **Candidate**.
3.  Enter your credentials and click **Sign In**.
4.  The system will store your session and redirect you to the appropriate dashboard view.

### Multi-Step Registration
1.  Click **Get Started Free**.
2.  Choose **I'm a Recruiter** or **I'm a Candidate**.
3.  **Step 1 (Basics)**: Enter name, email, and password. Click **Continue**.
4.  **Step 2 (Role Info)**: Enter company details, title, and team size. Click **Continue**.
5.  **Step 3 (Confirm)**: Agree to the terms and privacy checkboxes. Click **Get Started**.
6.  You will be automatically logged in and taken to onboarding.

---

## 3. Sidebar Navigation

Once inside the dashboard layout, use the sidebar to navigate the platform views:
- **Dashboard**: High-level overview of active jobs and talent distribution statistics.
- **Candidate Intelligence**: Sourcing table showing candidates, matching scores, and overall P-factors.
- **Hidden Gems**: Dedicated view showing candidates with high skill adjacency matches.
- **Talent Marketplace**: General search panel to query candidate profiles.
- **My Skill DNA**: Selected candidate visualization chart and AI explanation report.
- **Notifications**: System Alerts and re-match notifications.
